## MAKING A RESERVATION

### Step 1: Select Restaurant & Date

1. Click **"Book Now"** button
2. Search restaurant by name or cuisine
3. Choose date using calendar
4. Choose time from available slots

**Note:** Premium slots show 🌟 icon — these are exclusive to members.

### Step 2: Choose Party Size

1. Use **"+"** and **"-"** buttons to adjust guest count
2. Shows you instantly which tables have availability
3. Recommended tables appear first

**Available slots show:**
- 🟢 **Green:** Tables available
- 🔴 **Red:** Fully booked (join waiting list if interested)

### Step 3: Select Your Table

You now see all available tables:

| Table | Location | Capacity | Vibe | Price |
|-------|----------|----------|------|-------|
| 📍 Window 1 | By the window | 2 | Cozy, romantic | Premium |
| 📍 Center 3 | Main dining | 4 | Social, lively | Standard |
| 📍 Patio 2 | Outdoor | 6 | Relaxed | Standard |

**Selecting:** Click the table you prefer. The map/preview shows your view from that seat.

### Step 4: Special Requests

Add any preferences:

- **Dietary Requirements:** Vegetarian, halal, allergies
- **Occasion:** Birthday, anniversary, business meeting
- **Seating:** High chair needed, mobility assistance
- **Other:** Special music preference, surprise message

These are shared with the restaurant to enhance your experience.

### Step 5: Review & Confirm

Check:
- ✅ Date and time correct
- ✅ Party size accurate
- ✅ Table selected
- ✅ Special requests detailed

Then click **"Confirm Reservation"**

### Step 6: Confirmation

You'll see:
- Confirmation ID (reference number)
- Digital reservation in your dashboard
- Email confirmation (check spam folder if not received)
- SMS reminder (if enabled, sent 24 hours before)

**Save Your Confirmation ID** — you may need it at the restaurant.

## MANAGING YOUR BOOKINGS

### View Upcoming Reservations

1. Log in to your account
2. Click **"My Reservations"** or **"Dashboard"**
3. See all future bookings with:
   - Restaurant name
   - Date and time
   - Party size
   - Table location
   - Confirmation ID

### Modifying a Reservation

To **change date, time, or table:**

1. Find reservation in list
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Choose new options
4. Click **"Update Reservation"**
5. Confirmation is updated

**Restrictions:**
- Must modify at least 24 hours before reservation
- Changes subject to availability
- Table type may change if original not available

### Cancelling a Reservation

To **cancel your booking:**

1. Find reservation in list
2. Click **"Cancel"** button
3. Confirm cancellation
4. You'll receive confirmation email

**Cancellation Policy:**
- Cancel up to 48 hours before: **Full loyalty points refunded**
- Cancel 24-48 hours before: **50% points refunded**
- Cancel less than 24 hours: **No refund** (table reserved for you)
- No-show (don't arrive): **Membership warning** (may affect bookings)

**Important:** Repeated cancellations may affect your booking privileges. Be sure of your plans before confirming!

### View Your History

See all past dining experiences:

1. Click **"Reservation History"**
2. See restaurants you've visited
3. View dining dates and ratings

Your history helps us recommend new restaurants you might enjoy.

## FAQ

### Q: How far in advance can I book?

**A:** As a premium member, you can book up to **60 days ahead**. Regular members book 30 days ahead. You receive early access when restaurants open their calendars.

### Q: Do I need to prepay?

**A:** No. Your reservation is guaranteed without payment. Some restaurants may offer optional prepayment for additional discounts.

### Q: What if the restaurant cancels?

**A:** If a restaurant cancels your reservation, our concierge team will:
1. Immediately contact you
2. Offer alternative date/time at same restaurant
3. Or recommend similar restaurant nearby
4. Award bonus loyalty points for inconvenience

### Q: Can I bring more guests than originally booked?

**A:** You should notify the restaurant immediately. If seats are available, they'll usually accommodate. Update your reservation in the app to change party size, or contact support.

### Q: What happens if I'm late?

**A:** Most restaurants hold your table for **15 minutes**. If running late:
1. Call restaurant directly (number in confirmation)
2. Update app : click "Running Late"
3. Restaurant notified automatically

Being very late may result in cancellation of your reservation.

### Q: Can I transfer my reservation to someone else?

**A:** Reservations are personal to your account. If you can't attend:
- **Option 1:** Cancel and friend books new reservation
- **Option 2:** Contact concierge; we may transfer it for you
- **Option 3:** Find a friend to use your confirmation code (they won't earn your points)

### Q: How do I redeem my loyalty points?

**A:**
1. Go to **Profile → Loyalty**
2. Browse available rewards
3. Click reward you want
4. Points automatically deducted
5. Discount code sent to email or applied to next booking

### Q: What if I don't like my table after arriving?

**A:** If seated at wrong table or have concerns:
1. Politely notify restaurant staff
2. Explain the issue
3. They'll accommodate if possible
4. File feedback in app after dining
5. Restaurants take member concerns seriously

### Q: Can I see the restaurant interior before booking?

**A:** 
- Most restaurants have **"View Table"** option
- Shows photo from that specific table's vantage point
- See the vibe and ambiance
- Read other member reviews
- Click restaurant name for full details

### Q: How are other members reviewing restaurants?

**A:** After your reservation, you'll be invited to rate:
- **Food Quality** (1-5 stars)
- **Service** (1-5 stars)
- **Atmosphere** (1-5 stars)
- **Value for Money** (1-5 stars)
- **Written Review** (optional; share your experience)

Your honest feedback helps other members choose great restaurants.

### Q: Will the restaurant know I'm a premium member?

**A:** Yes. Premium members are marked in the system, and restaurants see your membership status. They often:
- Offer special welcome
- Provide complimentary items
- Upgrade table if available
- Add personal touches (celebration acknowledgment, etc.)

### Q: What's the cancellation policy again?

**A:**
- **More than 48 hours:** Free cancellation + full points refund
- **24-48 hours:** Free cancellation + 50% points refund
- **Less than 24 hours:** Cancellation allowed but no refund
- **No-show:** Membership status review; repeated no-shows may affect access

## TIPS FOR GREAT BOOKINGS

### ✅ Best Practices

✅ **Book during members-only hour** (first hour of daily release) for premium tables
✅ **Enable notifications** so you don't miss exclusive offers
✅ **Review restaurant details** before confirming (cuisine, dress code, noise level)
✅ **Add special requests** (allergies especially!) — restaurants appreciate clarity
✅ **Arrive 10 minutes early** — shows your commitment, restaurants appreciate it
✅ **Leave feedback** after dining — helps community and earns you bonus points

### ❌ Things to Avoid

❌ Don't book if you're not sure you can attend (cancellations affect your reputation)
❌ Don't change reservations last-minute unnecessarily (restaurants prepare specifically)
❌ Don't disclose confirmation code publicly (anyone could use your booking)
❌ Don't assume dietary needs are communicated — always confirm with restaurant
❌ Don't expect restaurants to hold table indefinitely (typically 15 min grace period)
