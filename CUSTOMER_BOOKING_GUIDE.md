## QUICK START

### 30-Second Overview

1. Find the restaurant online (via their website button or app)
2. Choose your date and party size
3. Pick a time slot and table
4. Enter your name, email, phone
5. Confirm — you're booked!

You'll receive an email confirmation immediately. That's it!

## STEP-BY-STEP BOOKING

### Step 1: Find the Restaurant

**Option A: Via Restaurant Website**
- Look for "**Book a Table**" button
- Clicking takes you to our booking system

**Option B: Direct Link**
- Ask restaurant for booking link
- Or search restaurant name in our app

**Option C: Using App**
- Search restaurant by name or cuisine
- Click "Book Now"

### Step 2: Choose Your Date

Use the date picker to select when you want to dine:

- You can book **up to 30 days in advance**
- Dates available depend on restaurant hours
- Grayed-out dates mean restaurant is closed that day

**Tip:** Weekend evenings book quickly — reserve early if you have a preferred time!

### Step 3: Select Party Size

Use **"−"** and **"+"** buttons to choose number of guests:

```
Guest Count: [−] 2 [+]
```

- Can book for any size from 1 to 20+ guests
- Available times depend on table sizes
- Larger parties may have fewer time slots

### Step 4: Choose Your Time

You'll see all available time slots:

```
Available Times:
17:00 🟢 Available  >>
17:30 🟢 Available  >>
18:00 🟢 Available  >>
18:30 🟢 Available  >>
19:00 🔴 Fully Booked (Join Waiting List?)
```

- 🟢 **Green:** Tables available
- 🔴 **Red:** Fully booked
- Click **"Join Waiting List"** for booked times (we'll notify if slot opens)

Select your preferred time and click **"Next"**

### Step 5: Pick Your Table

Now choose where you'd like to sit:

```
Window Seating — Romantic, great views (2-4 guests)
    Table W1: 2-person, quiet corner
    Table W2: 4-person, by the window
    
Main Dining — Lively, social atmosphere (2-8 guests)
    Table M1: 4-person, center of action
    Table M3: 6-person, private, quieter
    
Patio — Outdoor, relaxed (4-8 guests)
    Table P1: 6-person (weather permitting)
```

**Each table shows:**
- Location/vibe
- Capacity (how many people it seats)
- Special features (quiet, private, view, accessible, etc.)

Click the table you prefer. You'll see a preview of the view from that seat!

### Step 6: Add Special Requests

Tell the restaurant about your needs:

**Dietary Requirements:**
- Vegetarian
- Vegan
- Halal
- Kosher
- Gluten-free
- Allergies (please be specific)

**Occasion:**
- Birthday (restaurant may add special touches!)
- Anniversary
- Business meeting
- Special celebration

**Other Preferences:**
- High chair needed
- Wheelchair accessibility required
- Service dog accompanying
- Prefer quieter table
- Any other requests

The restaurant will read your requests and prepare accordingly.

### Step 7: Your Contact Information

Enter your details so restaurant can reach you:

```
First Name: [_____________]
Last Name:  [_____________]
Email:      [_____________]
Phone:      [_____________]
```

**Important:** Use phone number the restaurant can actually reach you on.

### Step 8: Confirm & Book

Review everything:
- ✅ **Date & Time** — Correct?
- ✅ **Party Size** — Right number of guests?
- ✅ **Table** — Selected?
- ✅ **Contact Info** — Accurate?
- ✅ **Special Requests** — Noted?

Click **"Confirm Reservation"**

### Step 9: Get Your Confirmation

You'll see:

```
✅ Booking Confirmed!

Confirmation ID: ABC123XYZ

Date: Saturday, March 22, 2026
Time: 7:00 PM
Party Size: 4 Guests
Table: Window Seating, Table W2
Restaurant: The Dining Club

📧 Confirmation email sent to: you@email.com
📱 SMS reminder will be sent 24 hours before
```

**Save your Confirmation ID** — bring it or have it ready when you arrive!

## AFTER YOU BOOK

### You'll Receive (Usually Within Minutes)

1. **Email Confirmation**
   - Includes: reservation details, confirmation ID, restaurant address
   - Check spam folder if not received
   - Forward to anyone joining you

2. **SMS Reminder (Optional)**
   - Sent 24 hours before your reservation
   - Includes: time, restaurant name, address
   - Quick link to modify reservation if needed

### Making Changes to Your Booking

**To change time, date, or party size:**

1. Click link in email
   — OR —
   Click "Manage Reservation" button on confirmation screen
   
2. You can:
   - Change date/time (if availability exists)
   - Change party size (within reason)
   - Add/update special requests
   - Change dining area preference

3. New confirmation sent immediately
4. Restaurant notified of change

**Important:** Changes must be made **at least 24 hours before** your reservation. Last-minute changes may not be available.

### Cancelling Your Reservation

**To cancel:**

1. Click cancellation link in email
   — OR —
   In app → Find Reservation → Click Cancel

2. Confirm cancellation
3. Confirmation email sent
4. Slot becomes available for others to book

**Cancellation impacts:**
- ✅ No penalty for cancellations 48+ hours before
- ⚠️ Restaurant disappointed if cancelled very late
- ⏰ Restaurant may have difficulty re-booking last-minute slots

**Best practice:** Cancel promptly if plans change so restaurant can seat others.

### What to Do When Arriving

**Arrive 10-15 minutes early:**
1. Enter through restaurant entrance
2. Tell host/hostess your name
3. Have confirmation ID ready (or show email on phone)
4. They'll seat you at your reserved table

**Show your confirmation ID** (found in email or on this app)

**If running late:**
1. Call restaurant directly (number in email confirmation)
2. Let them know you're on your way
3. Most restaurants hold table for **15 minutes**
4. Beyond 15 min, they may give table to waiting guests

## SPECIAL SITUATIONS

### Situation 1: All Times Are Fully Booked

**Option 1: Join the Waiting List**
- Click "Join Waiting List" for your preferred time
- If someone cancels, we'll text you immediately
- You'll have 15 minutes to confirm before slot goes to next person

**Option 2: Choose Different Time**
- Try earlier dinner (17:00-18:30 often available)
- Try later dinner (21:00+ may have slots)
- Try different day (weekdays busier than mid-week)

**Option 3: Contact Restaurant**
- Call restaurant directly (ask when they stop accepting online bookings)
- Phone reservations may still be available
- They might accommodate you as a walk-in

### Situation 2: You Want a Different Table Than Offered

First, try: **Click the different table** — if available, you can select it!

If that table:
- ❌ **Not available:** That size/time combo not available for that area
- Let restaurant know when you arrive (they'll do best to accommodate if possible)

### Situation 3: You're Coming With Large Group

**For 10+ guests:**
1. Book in sections if needed (example: 2 tables of 5)
   — OR —
2. Call restaurant directly
3. Explain group size
4. They may offer:
   - Private section/room
   - Special group menu
   - Fixed price arrangement

### Situation 4: Dietary Allergies (Very Important!)

**Please message restaurant directly:**
1. Book your reservation normally
2. Note allergies in "Special Requests" field
3. **Call restaurant** (phone number in confirmation) to confirm they noted it
4. Don't rely on email alone for serious allergies

Restaurants take allergies very seriously, but direct communication ensures they prepare correctly.

### Situation 5: Birthday or Special Celebration

**Tell us in Special Requests:**
- "Birthday celebration — Sarah's 30th!"
- "5th wedding anniversary"
- "Engagement party"
- Restaurants often add complimentary touches for occasions

**Pro tip:** Mention it early, then call restaurant day-of to double-check they're prepared.

### Situation 6: You Didn't Get a Confirmation Email

**Check:**
1. Spam/junk folder
2. Typo in email address during booking?

**If not found:**
1. Return to booking link
2. Click "Resend Confirmation"
3. Enter your email address
4. Or call restaurant directly

### Situation 7: You Want to Cancel Very Last Minute

**Within 24 hours of reservation:**
1. Call restaurant directly (best option)
2. Apologize for short notice
3. This gives them chance to offer to walk-ins
4. They'll appreciate the courtesy

## FAQ

### Q: Can I book without creating an account?

**A:** Yes! Guest bookings don't require an account. Just enter your contact info and you're done.

### Q: How far in advance can I book?

**A:** Up to **30 days ahead**. Premium members can book 60 days ahead.

### Q: What if I'm late?

**A:** 
- Call restaurant immediately (number in confirmation)
- Most hold for 15 minutes
- Beyond that, they may seat walk-in guests
- Arrive as soon as possible!

### Q: Can I modify my reservation?

**A:** Yes, if you do it **at least 24 hours before** your booking. After that, call restaurant directly.

### Q: What if I need to cancel?

**A:** From your confirmation email, click the cancellation link. The slot opens for others to book.

### Q: Do I need to pay to book?

**A:** No. Bookings are free. You pay at restaurant for food/drinks you consume.

### Q: What's your cancellation policy?

**A:** Cancellations are always welcome — just do it as soon as you know you can't make it. No penalties or fees.

### Q: Can I bring extra guests?

**A:** If you booked for 4 and 6 arrive, notify restaurant immediately upon arrival. They'll accommodate if possible, but no guarantee.

### Q: What if the restaurant cancels on me?

**A:** Unlikely, but if it happens:
- You'll get email/SMS notification
- We'll help find alternative restaurant nearby
- Restaurant may offer compensation for inconvenience

### Q: Can I book for someone else?

**A:** Yes. Just enter their contact info, but put your number as the callback number (so you know if there are changes).

### Q: How early should I arrive?

**A:** **10-15 minutes early** is ideal. Gives you time to:
- Find parking/enter restaurant
- Catch your breath
- Be ready to sit at your table exactly on time

### Q: What if I have a disability or accessibility needs?

**A:** 
- Note in "Special Requests" (e.g., "wheelchair accessible table needed")
- Call restaurant to confirm setup is adequate
- They're usually very accommodating

### Q: Can I see photos of the restaurant?

**A:** Most restaurant pages include:
- Interior photos
- Table preview photos
- Menu images
- Customer reviews
Click restaurant name for full details.

### Q: How do I know which table is right for me?

**Tips:**

| Want This | Choose This |
|-----------|-------------|
| Quiet, romantic | Private/corner tables |
| Lively, social | Center/main dining area |
| Views, outdoors | Patio/window seating |
| Accessible | Accessible tables (noted) |
| Private meeting | Private/corner, away from main action |
| Group celebration | Main area, larger table |

### Q: Do I need to confirm my reservation?

**A:** Confirmation emails ask you to click a link to "confirm." Do this within 24 hours if requested — it locks in your table.

### Q: My reservation disappeared. What happened?

**Possible reasons:**
1. Restaurant cancellation (you'll get email)
2. You didn't confirm when asked (within 24 hours)
3. System glitch (contact support)

**Solution:** Check email spam folder first, then call restaurant to verify.

### Q: Can I book multiple tables?

**A:** Yes. Make separate bookings for different tables if:
- Large group splitting across seating
- Want flexibility with different group members
- Planning private event

### Q: Do I get a loyalty rewards program?

**A:** Not automatically, but:
- **Sign up for email list** → get periodic offers
- **Join premium membership** → earn loyalty points
- Some restaurants have their own rewards (ask them!)

### Q: What time should I book for dinner?

**It depends:**
- **Early (5-6 PM):** Quieter, faster service, good for families
- **Peak (7-8:30 PM):** Most popular, lively, busier
- **Late (9 PM+):** Calmer again, slower service, feels exclusive

## HAVING PROBLEMS?

### Booking Won't Process

- Refresh page (might be temporary glitch)
- Try different time slot
- Clear browser cookies and try again
- Contact restaurant directly
- Email: `support@table-reservation.dev`

### Didn't Get Confirmation Email

- Check spam/junk folder
- Typo in email address?
- Ask restaurant to resend
- Call restaurant instead

### Restaurant Issue or Complaint

1. Note what happened (date, time, issue)
2. Contact restaurant directly (ideally immediately after experience)
3. If unresolved, email `support@table-reservation.dev` with details
4. We'll mediate and work toward resolution

### Account/Login Issues

- Forgot password? Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- Reset via email link
- Still having trouble? Contact support
