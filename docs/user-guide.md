## Overview

The Table Reservation System allows you to book a table at the restaurant directly from the website. There are three ways to make a reservation depending on your relationship with the restaurant:

| Flow | Who It's For | Account Required? | Key Features |
|------|-------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Guest** | First-time or casual diners | No | Quick 4-step booking with email confirmation |
| **Logged-In** | Registered customers | Yes (free) | Booking history, saved details, self-service cancellation |
| **Premium** | Loyalty/paid members | Yes (membership) | Interactive floor plan, priority tables, enhanced visual experience |

### How It Works

All three flows follow the same core process:

```
Select Date, Time & Party Size → Choose Your Table → Provide Contact Info → Review & Confirm
```

Your reservation is confirmed instantly, and a confirmation email is sent automatically.

## 1. Guest Reservation

This is the quickest way to book a table — no account or login required.

### Step 1: Select Date, Time & Party Size

1. Click the **"Book a Table"** button on the restaurant's website.
2. You will be taken to the reservation wizard.
3. **Choose your date** using the date picker.
   - Dates are available up to **30 days** in advance (configurable by the restaurant).
   - Only future dates are selectable.
4. **Select a time slot** from the available options.
   - The restaurant's operating hours are shown automatically.
   - Time slots that are fully booked will not appear.
5. **Set your party size** using the guest counter (+ / − buttons).
   - The maximum party size is set by the restaurant (default: **20 guests**).
   - Minimum party size is **1**.
6. Click **"Next"** to proceed.

> **Note:** You must book at least 1 hour in advance. The restaurant may adjust this policy.

### Step 2: Choose Your Table

1. A list of available tables that fit your party size will be displayed.
2. Each table shows:
   - **Table name/number** (e.g., "Table 5", "T12")
   - **Seating capacity** (e.g., "Seats 4")
   - **Floor area** (e.g., Main Dining, Patio, Private Room)
   - **Table type** (e.g., Booth, Standard, High-Top)
3. **Tap a table** to select it. The selected table will be highlighted.
4. Click **"Next"** to continue.

> **Tip:** Tables are sorted by capacity (smallest fitting table first) to ensure the best match for your group.

### Step 3: Enter Your Contact Information

1. Fill in the following fields:
   - **First Name** *(required)*
   - **Last Name** *(optional)*
   - **Email** *(required)* — Your confirmation email will be sent here
   - **Phone Number** *(required)*
   - **Special Requests** *(optional)* — e.g., high chair, birthday celebration, dietary needs
2. Click **"Review Booking"** to proceed.

### Step 4: Review & Confirm

1. Review all your booking details:
   - Date and time
   - Party size
   - Selected table and location
   - Your contact information
   - Any special requests
2. To make changes, click **"Edit"** next to any section to go back to that step.
3. When everything looks correct, click **"Confirm Reservation"**.

### What Happens Next

- Your reservation is **immediately confirmed** (status: Confirmed).
- A **confirmation email** is sent to the email address you provided, containing:
  - Restaurant name
  - Date and time
  - Party size
  - A unique **Confirmation ID** (first segment of the booking reference)
- The default reservation duration is **90 minutes** (set by the restaurant).
- You will be redirected to a **Booking Confirmed** page with all your details.

## 2. Logged-In User Reservation

Registered users enjoy a streamlined booking experience with saved preferences and reservation management.

### Creating an Account

> **Note:** The current system creates accounts during restaurant signup. Customer self-registration is planned for a future update. Contact the restaurant to create a customer account if needed.

### Making a Reservation

1. **Log in** to your account via the **"Login"** page.
2. Click **"Reserve"** or navigate to the reservation page.
3. The process follows the same 4 steps as a Guest reservation:
   - **Step 1:** Date & Time → **Step 2:** Table → **Step 3:** Contact Info → **Step 4:** Review
4. Your contact information (name, email, phone) may be pre-filled from your profile.
5. Review and confirm as usual.
6. You will be redirected to the **Booking Confirmed** page.

### Additional Features for Logged-In Users

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Reservation History** | View all your past and upcoming reservations from your dashboard |
| **Self-Service Cancellation** | Cancel upcoming reservations directly from your dashboard |
| **Dashboard Access** | See status of all your bookings at a glance |

## 3. Premium Member Reservation

Premium members have access to an enhanced, visually rich reservation experience.

### How to Access Premium Booking

1. **Log in** to your premium/loyalty account.
2. Navigate to the **Premium Reservation** page (accessible via the "Premium Reserve" option in the menu).

### The Premium Booking Experience

The Premium flow provides a richer, interactive interface with:

- **Interactive floor plan** — View the actual restaurant layout and choose your preferred table directly on the map.
- **Real-time availability** — Tables are color-coded: available (green), occupied (grey), or selected (highlighted).
- **Table details on click** — Click any table on the floor plan to see its capacity, location, type, and shape.
- **Priority selection** — Premium members may have access to reserved or exclusive seating areas.
- **Floor area switching** — Toggle between different restaurant sections (e.g., Main Dining, Patio, Bar).

### Booking Steps

1. **Select your date, time, and party size** (same as the standard flow).
2. **Choose your table** using the interactive floor plan:
   - Available tables are highlighted on the map.
   - Tap a table to select it and view its details in a tooltip.
   - Tables are shown with their actual shape (round, square, rectangle).
3. **Confirm your contact details** (pre-filled from your premium profile).
4. **Review and confirm** your reservation.
5. You will be redirected to a **Premium Booking Confirmed** page with all details.

## 4. Managing Your Reservations

### Viewing Upcoming Reservations *(Logged-In / Premium only)*

1. Log in and go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Your upcoming reservations are listed with:
   - Restaurant name
   - Date and time
   - Party size
   - Table assignment
   - Current status (Confirmed, Arriving, Seated, etc.)

### Cancelling a Reservation *(Logged-In / Premium only)*

1. From your Dashboard, locate the reservation you want to cancel.
2. Click **"Cancel"** next to the reservation.
3. Confirm the cancellation when prompted.
4. The reservation status changes to **Cancelled** and the table is freed for other guests.

> **Note:** You can only cancel reservations in **Pending** or **Confirmed** status. Reservations that are already **Seated**, **Completed**, or **No-Show** cannot be cancelled.

### Reservation Status Guide

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Reservation received, awaiting confirmation by staff |
| **Confirmed** | Reservation confirmed — your table is secured |
| **Arriving** | You're expected to arrive shortly |
| **Seated** | You have been seated at your table |
| **Completed** | Your visit is finished |
| **Cancelled** | Reservation was cancelled |
| **No-Show** | You did not arrive for your reservation |

### Guest Reservation Changes

If you booked as a guest (without an account) and need to modify or cancel your reservation, please **contact the restaurant directly** by phone. Guest reservations cannot be managed online.

## 5. Account Management

### Logging In

1. Go to the restaurant's website and click **"Login"**.
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. Click **"Sign In"**.
4. You will be directed to your dashboard.

### Forgot Password

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot Password?"**.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. A password reset link will be sent to your email.
4. Click the link and set a new password.

> **Security Note:** For your protection, the system does not reveal whether an email address is registered. You will always see a success message.

## 6. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**
A: Up to 30 days in advance by default. The restaurant may adjust this window.

**Q: What is the maximum party size?**
A: The default maximum is 20 guests, but this is set by each restaurant and may vary.

**Q: How long is my table reserved for?**
A: The default reservation duration is 90 minutes. The restaurant may adjust this.

**Q: Will I receive a confirmation?**
A: Yes. A confirmation email is sent immediately after booking, regardless of which flow you use. The email includes your Confirmation ID.

**Q: Can I choose a specific table?**
A: Yes. During the booking process, you select from available tables. Premium members can use the interactive floor plan for a visual selection experience.

**Q: What happens if I don't show up?**
A: The restaurant may mark your reservation as **No-Show**. Repeated no-shows may affect future booking privileges.

**Q: Can I modify my reservation after booking?**
A: Logged-in and Premium users can cancel from their dashboard. To modify details (date, time, party size), please cancel and rebook, or contact the restaurant directly.

**Q: Is my personal information safe?**
A: Yes. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Contact details are only used for reservation communication.

## 7. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| No time slots appear | All tables may be booked for that date/time. Try a different date or time. |
| No tables available for my party size | The restaurant may not have tables large enough. Try splitting into smaller groups or contact the restaurant. |
| Confirmation email not received | Check your spam/junk folder. Ensure you entered the correct email address. |
| Cannot cancel a reservation | Only **Pending** or **Confirmed** reservations can be cancelled. If your reservation is **Seated** or **Completed**, contact the restaurant. |
| Page not loading | Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. |
